What is GoodRx?

GoodRx is a free service that negotiates lower drug and supply prices with pharmacies. No insurance needed — just show the coupon at checkout.

🆓
Completely Free
GoodRx is free to use. No membership, no subscription, no catch. Just lower prices.
🏪
Works Everywhere
Accepted at over 70,000 pharmacies including CVS, Walgreens, Walmart, and Costco.
📱
Show Your Phone
Get the GoodRx app, search your supply, show the coupon at the pharmacy. Done.
