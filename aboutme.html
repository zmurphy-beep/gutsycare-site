Our mission

We exist to make ostomy life
easier, cheaper, and less lonely.

The ostomy community is one of the most underserved groups in healthcare tech. Over 800,000 Americans live with an ostomy — and millions more worldwide — yet the tools available to them are outdated, fragmented, and built by people who don't understand what they actually need.

Gutsy Care was built to fix that. We're creating a single platform where ostomates can find affordable supplies, track their health, navigate their insurance benefits, locate nearby restrooms, and connect with a community that truly understands.

No more hunting across 10 different websites. No more overpaying at retail. No more unused supplies going to waste while someone else goes without. Just one app that has your back.
